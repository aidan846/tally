Usage

Ask. Calculate. Keep going.

Tally is a flexible scratchpad for the small calculations that come up every day. Type one thought per line and see the answer as you go.

01

Write calculations naturally

Use normal words, symbols, functions, parentheses, constants, and scientific notation. Tally understands the question, not just a rigid formula.

Tally
sum of 72 and 54 minus 3294.00
(15% of 240) + 1854.00
sin(pi / 2)1.00
1200000 scientific1.20e+6
